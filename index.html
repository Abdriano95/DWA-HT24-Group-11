Memory Game Instructions

Objective

The goal of the memory game is to find all matching pairs of cards. The game is won when all pairs have been matched. Your goal as a player to have as low highscore as possible.

How to Play

  1. Start the Game: When you open the game, you will see a grid of face down cards.
  2. Flip Two Cards: Click on any card to flip it over and reveal the symbol or letter on the other side. Click on a second card to flip it over as well.
  3. Check for a Match: If the symbols on the two flipped cards match, they remain face up. If they do not match, after a short delay, both cards will flip back over to their face down positions.
  4. Continue Playing: Repeat steps 2 and 3, flipping two cards at a time, until all pairs have been found.
  5. Winning the Game: The game is over when all pairs of cards have been matched and are face up. Congratulations, youâ€™ve won the game!

Tips and Strategies

  • Remember the Cards: Pay close attention to the symbols or letters on the cards you have flipped over. Try to remember their positions to match pairs more effectively.
  • Take Your Time: There is no time limit, so take your time to memorize card positions.
  • Concentration: The key to winning is concentration. Try to focus and avoid distractions.

Additional Features

  • Restarting the Game: If you want to play again, refresh the browser page to shuffle the cards and start a new game.
